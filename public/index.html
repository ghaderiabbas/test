How to apply to EDMX?

To apply to the doctoral program in Materials Science and Engineering, the candidate is requested to complete and submit an online application. Beforehand, we encourage you to visit our web site to get an idea of ongoing research in Materials at EPFL and to go to the Doctoral School homepage. The deadlines for applications are April 15 and December 15.
